The approach

Build the map before memorizing the details.

Students often struggle not because they're incapable, but because the material was presented as disconnected facts. We build structure first, what matters, how ideas connect, and how to reason through unfamiliar problems, then practice until it sticks.

  • Sessions follow your exact syllabus and textbook
  • Concepts explained before formulas and procedures
  • Active practice and explaining ideas in your own words
  • Study strategies that carry across future courses
